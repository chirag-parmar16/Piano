How to Use

Welcome to the Simple Beginner Piano! You can play notes by clicking on the piano keys with your mouse or by using the corresponding keys on your computer keyboard as shown in the key map above.

This piano includes three octaves of notes (C3 to B5). The white keys are the natural notes and the black keys are the sharps/flats.

Use the keyboard keys as shown in the chart above to play different octaves:

  • Bottom row (Z-M): First octave (C3-B3)
  • Top row (Q-U): Second octave (C4-B4)
  • Upper row (I-Enter): Third octave (C5-B5)

Have fun practicing!
